**Description**

Using Microsoft Excel, I analysed a multi-channel marketing campaign's performance, tracking the funnel from impressions to revenue across social, email, search, influencer, and display channels. The goal was to identify the most effective and affordable channels, compare channel performance, and help the company make an informed decision by optimizing budget allocation based on KPI analysis. Ultimately, this analysis is to guides the business on the most effective allocation of marketing investments to maximize returns.


**Business questions**

1.	which of the top 10 campaigns yielded the greatest return on investment (ROI)?
2.	which channels are responsible for generating the highest overall revenue?
3.	on which platform or through which method is the sales funnel most effective at converting impressions into completed conversions?
4.	are there identifiable seasonal trends or a specific month in which campaign performance is notably higher?


**Objectives**

The analysis aimed to:
1.	Pinpoint top-performing campaigns based on ROI.
2.	Evaluate channel efficiency.
3.	Identify seasonal trends and potential campaign fatigue.


**Data Cleaning and transformation**

To ensure accurate insights, the dataset was thoroughly prepared. This involved formatting dates and currency values, resolving missing data, duplicates, and empty spaces. I then derived crucial metrics like CTR, cost per lead, ROI, conversion rate, and campaign duration by creating new calculated columns. The result is a clean, standardized, and transformed dataset ready for in-depth analysis.

**Data Analysis**

. which of the top 10 campaigns yielded the greatest return on investment (ROI)?

Critical assessment of the 'Top 10 Campaigns by ROI' visualization indicates a convergence at the highest performance tier, with multiple campaigns exhibiting an equivalent ROI of 2. This presents a limitation in discerning the absolute top performer based solely on this metric. Despite the limitation, a channel-specific slicer highlighted that the 'Display' marketing channel was instrumental in driving the greatest number of campaigns achieving this elevated ROI. The ROI was calculated correctly based on the following formular ROI=(Revenue-Cost)/Cost

•	which channels are responsible for generating the highest overall revenue?

Analysis of the 'Total Revenue by Channel' chart reveals that the Display channel is the leading revenue generator, with approximately $10.6 million. This channel's significant revenue is further supported by its contribution to about 20% of total conversions, highlighting a strong correlation between high volume and high revenue.

•	on which platform or through which method is the sales funnel most effective at converting impressions into completed conversions?

The Total Impressions and Conversions by Channel data highlights a key insight: despite lower impression and conversion volumes from Search and Social channels, the Influencer channel achieves significantly higher conversions relative to its impressions. This suggests that the Influencer channel is the most effective for maximizing impression-to-conversion rates.

•	are there identifiable seasonal trends or a specific month in which campaign performance is notably higher?

There are identifiable seasonal trends in campaign performance, particularly visible in the monthly leads and conversions generation analysis and monthly cost and revenue generation analysis graphs. In leads and conversions graph, sum of conversions are significantly higher in the month of April, July, August and October. There is a dip in the month of February, march, September and November and a slight increase in the January, May, June and December. However, in mostly cost and revenue analysis, April, May and October were the months where highest revenue were generated.

**KEY RECOMMENDATION**

Increase investment and focus more on influencer which shows highest conversion rate. Explore strategies to scale successful influencer campaigns and identify more influencers who can replicate this high conversion performance. 

As email and display channels contributed to the highest successful conversion, continue to invest in and optimize Email and Display campaigns. Ensure strong calls to action and compelling content to maximize revenue. Explore opportunities to increase the volume of impressions or engagement within these channels, as they are proven revenue drivers.

On seasonal performance peak, Plan and allocate increased marketing budgets and campaign efforts during these peak months to maximize results. Consider running specific seasonal promotions or campaigns during these periods. Conversely, review performance during lower months to see if strategies can be adjusted or if these months are better suited for different types of campaigns (e.g., brand awareness).
